
# ⚽ Sports Store Analytics Dashboard

### Project Overview

The Sports Store Analytics Dashboard is designed to provide a comprehensive view of sales performance, product profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends for a sports retail business.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, identify growth opportunities, evaluate product performance, and make data-driven decisions through interactive visualizations and actionable insights.

The analysis covers:
Revenue Performance
Profitability Analysis
Product Performance
Customer Segmentation
Acquisition Channel Effectiveness
Regional Performance
Sales Trends

## 📊 Dashboard 1: Sales Performance Overview
### Purpose

This page provides a high-level overview of the business's financial performance and sales trends.

### Key Metrics
- Total Revenue: ₹11.22M
- Total Profit: ₹5.93M
- Profit Margin: 52.81%
- Yearly Revenue Growth: 30.1%

### Key Insights
- Revenue increased consistently from 2021 to 2025.
- Profit growth closely follows revenue growth, indicating healthy business performance.
- Channel contribution is balanced, reducing dependency on a single acquisition source.
- Monthly revenue trends reveal seasonal sales peaks during specific periods.

### Business Impact

This page enables executives to quickly assess overall business health and identify revenue growth patterns.

## 📦 Dashboard 2: Product Analysis
### Purpose

This page evaluates product categories, subcategories, pricing strategies, and revenue contributions.

### Key Findings
- Kits emerged as the highest revenue-generating category.
- Boots contributed approximately ₹4.22M in revenue, making them a major profit driver.
- The top two product subcategories account for over 70% of total revenue.
- Product pricing varies significantly across categories, influencing profit margins.

### Business Recommendations
- Increase inventory for top-performing products.
- Focus marketing campaigns on high-revenue categories.
- Review low-performing products for optimization or discontinuation.
- Introduce premium pricing strategies for high-demand products.

### Business Impact

This analysis helps inventory managers and product teams maximize profitability through better product portfolio decisions.

## 👥 Dashboard 3: Customer Segmentation Analysis
### Purpose

This page provides insights into customer behavior, acquisition channels, customer retention, and revenue contribution.

### Key Findings
- Customer base increased by 28.1% during the analysis period.
- Casual Fans represent the largest customer segment.
- Organic Search is the highest revenue-generating acquisition channel.
- Customers with a tenure greater than two years contribute the highest revenue.
- Web traffic exceeds Mobile App traffic, indicating stronger web engagement.

### Business Recommendations
- Strengthen SEO and Organic Search strategies.
- Launch loyalty programs targeting long-term customers.
- Improve customer retention campaigns for new customers.
- Enhance mobile application experience to increase engagement.

### Business Impact

Understanding customer behavior enables targeted marketing campaigns and improved customer retention strategies.
